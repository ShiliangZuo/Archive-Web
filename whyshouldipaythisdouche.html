Why should I Pay?

By paying, it means you enjoyed the game and/or you want to support me make bigger and better games on more platforms for more people to enjoy. I prefer this rather than ad revenue cuz with ad revenue, I feel like the audience doesn't really have a choice. I would rather the audience willingly show support instead of ad revenue. I am new to Paypal, so there might be problems, but you should be able to choose the amount you want to pay.

为什么我要付款?

付款是对游戏及其开发者的支持。有你的支持，我可以在更多平台开发更有趣的游戏，让更多人享受到。 我喜欢这种方式而非广告盈利，因为我感觉广告对于玩家有强迫的性质。我更希望玩家能主动表达支持，而非靠广告获利。 我刚开通Paypal，还不太熟悉，不过你应该能够自己选择付款的金额。请注意是 $ 还是 ¥.
